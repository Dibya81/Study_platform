The Problem

Traditional AI tools rely on a single model which can produce hallucinations and unreliable answers. Users have no way to verify the correctness of responses.

The Solution

Consensus Engine introduces a multi-model AI verification system. Instead of trusting one AI model, our system compares answers from multiple AI models and uses a judge model to determine the most accurate response.
